Intelligent Advisory Portfolios (IAP) offer a diverse array of pre-packaged equity portfolios guided by Registered Investment Advisors (RIAs), enabling investors to participate in the equity markets without the need for extensive stock research, market analysis, or number crunching.


  • Why consider investing in these portfolios?
  • Multiple Product Options: Choose from a variety of products tailored to suit your investment preferences.
  • ROBO Managed or Process-driven: Benefit from portfolios managed by automated systems or meticulously designed processes, supplemented with advisor expertise.
  • Dynamic or Static Portfolios: Decide between portfolios that adapt to market conditions dynamically or maintain a stable composition.
  • Power of Discretion: Retain control over every investment recommendation, ensuring alignment with your financial goals and risk tolerance.
  • Convenient Online Investing: Access and invest in IAP portfolios conveniently through online platforms.
  • Flexible Investment Modes: Opt for either lump sum or systematic investment plan (SIP) modes based on your investment strategy and preferences.
  • Continuous Portfolio Tracking: Enjoy 24x7 monitoring of your portfolio's performance and progress.
